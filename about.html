About

The Meaning of Simply 8

Simply 8 is more than just a band. We have a goal in mind writing our music. Approximately 92% of all popular music contains profanities in the lyrics and meaning. A shocking 8% do not. Simply 8 wants to write meaningful clean music in order to be in that 8%. We believe it is this that makes us fresh, interesting, and most importantly, different.

Band Members

Gabe Mukobi

Gabriel Mukobi was born in Columbus, Ohio, and moved to Camas, Washington, in 2003. He plays guitar in Simply 8 and has been playing the instrument for 7 years. Additionally, Gabe has played French horn for 5 years and currently plays it in the Camas High School wind ensemble, symphonic band, brass choir, and marching band. He is also involved in the CHS Jazz One jazz band where he plays guitar. His favorite guitarists are Stevie Ray Vaughan and Jimi Hendrix.

Hayden Kane

Rachel Higgins

Rachel Higgins was born and raised in Camas, Washington. She grew up singing in the church as well as playing music in school. Along with singing, Rachel plays the trumpet, mandolin, and the ukulele. Rachel enjoys performing and entertaining. She hopes that Simply 8's music will have a positive impact on people.

Soroush Badiei

Zephry Lyne

Born 7/19/1997, Zephry has been involved in music for 12 years playing all sorts of instruments ranging from violin, trumpet, euphonium, and piano and has been playing guitar for six years. He has played in his high school jazz band, his Sunday morning church services and now guitar with Simply 8.
