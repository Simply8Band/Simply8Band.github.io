SIMPLY 8
(Not just another rock band)

Welcome to the official website of the band Simply 8! We are a band of 5 teenaged musicians from Camas, WA.

We write our own songs (we just recorded an EP of 5 of those songs!) and play covers in performances as well. Our songs our mainly of the rock genre.

On this website, you can find a calendar displaying everything that we have scheduled so far, a gallery of photos and videos taken throughout our exploits, our album list and song lyrics, information about the band, ways to contact us, our social media, and a PayPal donation link.

Feel free to have a look around, or hit the Facebook logo to go to our Facebook page. There we post rather frequently with new pictures, recently scheduled gigs, reminders for upcoming events, and general announcements. If you like us, feel free to like and/or share our Facebook page, as it is very much appreciated!
