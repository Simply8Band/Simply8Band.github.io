Purchase Our Music

Digital Copy

Our EP is for sale on the internet! If you want to purchase a digital version of Simply 8's first official release, the Simply 8 EP, you can currently get it on one of the following sites:

iTunes
Spotify
Google Play
BandCamp
RouteNote Direct

Our online distribution partner is in the proccess of sending our music out to more vendors, and it should soon be available on YouTube, SoundCloud, Groove, emusic, Shazam, Deezer, Amazon Music, and many more soon.

Physical CD Copy

Right now, we are in the proccess of getting physical CD copies of our EP replicated, printed, packaged, and shipped. If you want to purchase a physical CD copy Simply 8's first official release, the Simply 8 EP, look for a table at one of our concerts with a nice person selling T-shirts and other merchandise. In a short time from now, we will also have CDs for sale there for $8.

Additionally, once we start the main production proccess for our CD, you will be able to buy a physical CD copy of the EP online and have it shipped to you through our CD distribution partner. Check back here soon for a link!

Merchandise

T-Shirts

Simply 8 is selling T-shirts with the Simply 8 EP album artwork and the text, "Simply 8." The shirts come in both grey and red and in sizes from small to extra large and everything inbetween.

To purchase a t-shirt for $15, look for a table at one of our concerts selling them. The t-shirts will be on display on the table.
